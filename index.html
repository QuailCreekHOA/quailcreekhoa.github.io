Welcome to the Neighborhood!

Quail Creek is located in the Ames Lake area of Redmond, WA.

The official Quail Creek Homeowners Association (QCHOA) is comprised of 40 homes in the Quail Creek neighborhood. The QCHOA Board of Directors handles questions and concerns for these homeowners, and manages the finances and maintenance of the front entrance. The Board also plans activities dedicated to preserving homeowner value while helping to make everyone in our warm and friendly neighborhood feel at home.

On this site you can find QCHOA documents, links to local resources, and information regarding QCHOA's project approval process.

If you want to meet neighbors and/or have ideas on how to better our community, contact the board to get involved. All Quail Creek neighbors (HOA and non-HOA) are welcome and encouraged to take an active role in enhancing the beauty and warmth of our community!
